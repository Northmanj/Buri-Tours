FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about Kenya travel and booking with Buri Tours in Nairobi.

Best time to visit Maasai Mara?

July–October is popular for the Great Migration, while January–March often offers excellent wildlife sightings with fewer crowds.

Cost of a Kenya safari?

Pricing varies by park, season, accommodation, and transport. We offer affordable packages and can tailor trips to your budget.
